Core Logic

The core philosophy behind Strategy 1 is that market makers often manipulate price above or below key swing points to trap retail traders before reversing the trend. This is commonly known as a Liquidity Sweep or "Stop Hunt".

We wait for the price to aggressively push into a level of interest, followed by an immediate and powerful rejection. This rejection must form a SuperEngulfing Candleâ€”a candle that completely engulfs the body and wicks of the previous candle, closing near its extreme.

To avoid false signals during low volatility or choppy markets, we overlay a Daily Bias Filter. If the daily timeframe is bullish, we only take long signals. If bearish, we only look for shorts.
